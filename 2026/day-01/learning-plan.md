# My 90 Days DevOps Learning Plan

## Current Level

I am a fresher transitioning into the DevOps and Cloud domain. I have basic knowledge of Linux and AWS fundamentals, and I am currently building my skills through hands-on practice, projects, and continuous learning.

---

## My Understanding of DevOps

DevOps is a combination of culture, practices, and tools that enables organizations to deliver applications faster and more reliably. It promotes collaboration between Development and Operations teams while emphasizing automation, continuous integration, continuous delivery (CI/CD), monitoring, and continuous improvement.

For me, DevOps is not just about learning tools; it is about developing the mindset of automation, ownership, problem-solving, and continuous learning.

---

## Why I Am Learning DevOps and Cloud

The technology industry is rapidly moving toward cloud-native applications, automation, and scalable infrastructure. DevOps and Cloud Engineering play a critical role in making this possible.

I am learning DevOps because I enjoy solving technical challenges and want to build a career where I can work with modern technologies such as AWS, Docker, Kubernetes, Jenkins, and Terraform. My goal is to gain practical experience, work on real-world projects, and become a skilled DevOps Engineer.

---

## My End Goal

My primary goal is to become a confident and job-ready DevOps Engineer with strong practical knowledge of cloud platforms, automation, containerization, and infrastructure management.

I want to contribute to real-world projects, continuously improve my technical skills, and build a successful career in Cloud and DevOps Engineering.

---
